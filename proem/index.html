Yo. I'm Ethan.

After years of seeing all of my closest friends manage their own blogs (see: 1, 2, 3), I finally found it within myself to take a stab at creating my own. I intend to write out my thoughts on any amusement, obsession, lust, or other passion that possesses my mind and yearns to escape.

Those who decide to use leisure as a means of mental development, who love good music, good books, good pictures, good plays, good company, good conversation - what are they? They are the happiest people in the world.

- William Lyon Phelps

I fancy a lot of different things, but I think most of what I'll write about here will be about movies/tv, anime/manga, books, or, from time to time, some strange deep-cut math thing I had rattling around in my head. I'm down bad for any good high fantasy series, and also any bad high fantasy series, but I also enjoy science fiction and sometimes historical fiction. I also really enjoy board games and card games, mostly for the social aspect.
